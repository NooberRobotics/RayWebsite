Design

The mechanical design consists of two major components, the chassis and arm mechanism. The chassis is comprised of laser cut acrylic, wherein the motors, sensors, and electronics will be contained. The arm system can be further simplified into two major components, the claw and arm. The claw consists of a three gear and motor system to hold the passengers. The arm pivots about the chassis, which is controlled via another motor.
